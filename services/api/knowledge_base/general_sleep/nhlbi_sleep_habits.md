# Source
NHLBI sleep habits guidance

# Intended Use
General lifestyle education for sleep health.

# Summary
- Healthy sleep habits include maintaining a regular schedule, allowing enough time for sleep, winding down before bed, and being deliberate about light exposure, activity, meals, caffeine, and alcohol.
- These are low-risk education topics that fit well with an app-based coaching layer because they support symptom tracking and trend interpretation without drifting into diagnosis or treatment.
