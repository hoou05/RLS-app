# Source
Project-local RLS user education templates

# Intended Use
Reusable user-safe language for mild or possible RLS-style patterns.

# Summary
- Encourage users to log when discomfort begins, whether it is most prominent during rest, whether movement gives partial or complete relief, and how often symptoms delay sleep or fragment the night.
- Users can also be asked to note whether symptoms spread beyond the legs, whether they are getting more frequent, and whether daytime concentration, mood, or functioning is affected.
- The preferred user-facing tone is: possible feature match, educational explanation, low-risk lifestyle support, and referral when symptoms are persistent or clinically disruptive.
