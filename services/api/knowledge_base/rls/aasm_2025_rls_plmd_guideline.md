# Source
AASM 2025 RLS and PLMD clinical practice guideline

# Intended Use
Clinical background only. Do not output medication choices, dosing, iron protocols, or treatment plans directly to users.

# Summary
- The guideline reinforces that RLS and PLMD management begins with accurate clinical characterization, attention to symptom burden, and careful separation from mimics or contributing comorbid conditions.
- In an education-facing agent, the useful high-level takeaways are that persistent sleep disruption, clinically meaningful daytime impairment, pregnancy, renal disease, anemia or low iron states, neuropathy, and medication-related concerns all justify clinician involvement rather than self-treatment.
- Drug names, iron strategies, dosing, and formal treatment algorithms should remain backend context only and should not be surfaced as user instructions in this app.
